Project Orion is a next-generation AI initiative developed by NexusCorp. NexusCorp was founded by Dr. Elena Vance in 2018. Dr. Elena Vance previously worked as the Chief Scientist at Aether Technologies. 

Project Orion utilizes a specialized hardware cluster known as the Quantum Mesh. The Quantum Mesh is manufactured by Matrix Foundry. Matrix Foundry is located in Austin, Texas.

Aether Technologies developed the core algorithm behind Project Orion, known as Symbiotic Inference. Symbiotic Inference requires high-bandwidth memory architectures provided exclusively by Matrix Foundry.
